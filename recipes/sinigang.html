Sinigang

A steaming bowl of soup, meat, and vegetables, with the sour broth bearing a perfect blend of flavors-that's the taste of home.

It comes together really quickly, and the flavor is just perfect.

Ingredients

  • 8 cups of water
  • 2 medium-sized red onions, quartered
  • 4 medium-sized tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 kilo pork, cubed
  • 1 pack (44 grams) knorr sinigang sa sampaloc mix with gabi
  • 3 pieces taro (gabi), peeled and quartered
  • 2 pieces radish (labanos), peeled and sliced
  • 1 bunch string beans (sitaw), cut into 1-inch long pieces
  • 6 to 8 pieces okra
  • 2 pieces eggplants, sliced
  • 3 pieces green chili (siling tagalog)
  • 4 tablespoons fish sauce (patis)
  • 1 bunch kangkong (water spinach) leaves, tender stems pinched off

Recipe Instructions

  1. In a large pot, pour the water. Add the onions and tomatoes.

  2. Add the pork and let it simmer for 30 minutes. Remove the scum from the pork.

  3. Pour in one pack of Knorr Sinigang sa Sampaloc Mix with Gabi.

  4. Add the taro and radish. Let simmer for 15 minutes or until tender.

  5. Put the string beans, fish sauce, okra, eggplant, and green chili.

  6. Cook until the pork is tender. Add in the kangkong leaves before serving.
